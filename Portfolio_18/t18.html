A Passion for Pediatric Care

With over 12 years of experience, Dr. Chen combines medical expertise with a warm, personal approach. She believes in building lasting relationships with families, creating a foundation of trust to ensure every child receives the best possible care. She is a graduate of Columbia University's College of Physicians and Surgeons and is board-certified in Pediatrics.

Our Pediatric Services

We offer a full spectrum of services to support your child's health and development.

Well-Child Visits

Routine check-ups to monitor growth, development, and administer vaccinations.

Sick Visits & Acute Care

Prompt diagnosis and treatment for common childhood illnesses and injuries.

Developmental Screenings

Assessing developmental milestones to ensure your child is on the right track.

A Welcoming Space for Children

Our clinic is designed to be a comfortable and friendly environment for children and parents alike. From our bright waiting area to our child-friendly examination rooms, we strive to make every visit a positive experience.
